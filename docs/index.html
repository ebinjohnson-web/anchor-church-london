About Anchor Church London

A place to worship, grow, and belong.

Anchor Church London is a Christian church in London, Ontario. We are a family that worships together, grows together, and learns the good news of Jesus Christ.

Whether you are new to church, returning after some time away, or looking for a church family, we would be glad to welcome you.

What we value

  • Jesus Christ as Lord and Saviour
  • The Bible as God’s Word for faith and life
  • Prayer as dependence on God
  • Fellowship as life together in Christ
  • Mission as sharing God’s love with others
